Barceptionist wanted

Noosa Council is seeking an innovative and tech-savvy provider of a barista/reception (Barception) service for its Digital Hub in Peregian Beach.

In keeping with the cutting-edge nature of the Digital Hub facility, this is a unique entrepreneurial opportunity for one or more individuals to run a coffee kiosk whilst also providing a reception service from the Hub’s Co-Op canteen.

In return for providing barista, reception and related services, the service provider will be granted a license to operate the coffee kiosk and will retain all of the proceeds from coffee sales.
