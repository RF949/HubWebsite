The facility is built over two levels with approximately 750m2 of commercial space to accommodate a range of private office spaces and open co-working areas.



Floorplan

Ground Floor

Approximately 193.5m2 of potential office space. 2 separate meeting rooms. Secure main entrances at front and back with swipe key access allowing 24/7 secure building access. Private secure access to individual leased areas.

Upper floor

Approximately 321.5m2 of potential office space. Lift to upper floor. Flexible meeting room space enabling configuration as 1,2 or 3 separate meeting rooms. Outdoor, undercover balconies and decks with sweeping views of the Noosa Hinterland. Social deck area.
